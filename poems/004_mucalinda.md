<pre class="stanza">
dear darkness,
how i miss your cool embrace.
let me wipe the sun 
shine from your face.

i see the thorns of a thousand 
children's roses on your grave.
it's just the breaking of the dawn
and not a fall from grace.


i cast the shadow.
i bring the shade.

</pre>
